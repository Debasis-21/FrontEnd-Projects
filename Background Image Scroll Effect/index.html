Welcome to Rajasthan

Rajasthan literally means "The Land of Kings". The State of Rajasthan was formed on 30 March 1949 when the states of the Rajputana Agency of the restwhile Indian Empire were merged into the new Indian Union. Its capital and largest city is Jaipur.

Jaipur is known as the Pink City, due to dominant colour scheme of its buildings. On 6 July 2019, UNESCO World Heritage Committee inscribed Jaipur the "Pink City of India" among its World Heritage Sites. The city is also home to the UNESCO World Heritage Sites Amer Fort and Jantar Mantar.
